# WSM 2025

Analysis of the 2025 World's Strongest Man finals re-scored under 7 different real-world scoring systems.

← [Back to home](../README.md) · [WSM 2026](wsm2026.md)

## TL;DR

- **Final result:** Rayno Nel beat Tom Stoltman **47-46.5** under the current WSM Linear scoring — by half a point.
- **Under every other system tested**, Stoltman wins. He had more event wins (1 outright + 1 tied) and Nel relied on consistency.
- This was even more system-sensitive than WSM 2026. Nel's win is a tie-averaging artifact — he never won an event outright but never finished worse than 3rd, and the linear 1.11x system rewards that pattern just barely enough.
